The project is mainly aimed at providing a most secured and user friendly online voting system. • Internet voting will allow the casting of electronic ballots from virtually any location around the world. • Elections may be organized in many different ways. Paper-based elections make use of paper ballots, while automated elections make use of some kind of voting machines that automate the voting and/or tabulation procedures. • When the voting machines are actually implemented by computers, we talk about electronic voting. • Online voting systems are much more formal than online polling systems, because they seek (or should seek) to accurately reflect the voters' preferences. • The present report focuses on online (electronic) voting systems. If the network used is the Internet, this fact may be emphasized by using the term Internet voting instead.
